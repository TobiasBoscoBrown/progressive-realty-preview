Meet your broker

Three decades in one valley. That's the whole advantage.

I founded Progressive Realty Corporation in 2008 after years with Realty World, Coldwell Banker, John L. Scott and RE/MAX. Since getting my license in 1991 I've closed close to 500 transactions, represented 64 separate builders and worked alongside seven developers across the Treasure Valley.

That history means I've seen this market through every cycle. I'll tell you what a home is really worth, where the risk hides in a contract, and when to walk away. No franchise script, no pressure, just judgment you can lean on.
